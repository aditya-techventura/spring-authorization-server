Generate Keystore and Truststore
--------------------------------
java -jar java-spiffe-helper-0.7.0.jar -c helper.conf

Export Root Certificate
-----------------------
openssl pkcs12 -in ./pki/spiffe-truststore.p12 -out ./pki/spiffe-root-ca.pem
** Enter password: secret

Display Root Certificate
-----------------------
openssl x509 -in ./pki/spiffe-root-ca.pem -text

** Add Root Certificate to keychain in Mac
